Kanye West x adidas Originals YEEZY Season 1 Lookbook

It’s hard to say if the fever pitch of YEEZY Season 1 is beginning to diminish or if it’s only just broken the ice. Ever since Kanye West presented his adidas Originals collection at New York Fashion Week, the fashion world has been fixated on his every move. Fans clamoured to get their hands on a pair of Yeezy Boosts, and sought out any information they could regarding pricing of what was supposed to be entry level clothing goods. His whirlwind tour included an interview with Complex which offered a reflection on the show, prior impressions and an outlook of what was to come. Leaked images of the “Season” lookbook surfaced a few days ago, but now an official version shot by fine-art photographer Jackie Nickerson has appeared on seasonzine.com, and provides an in-depth look at the YEEZY Season 1 collection.
